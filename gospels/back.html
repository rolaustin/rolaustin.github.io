每日圣经经文 / Daily Bible Verses - 365 Days

一月 / January

第1天 / Day 1

  • English: “For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.” - Jeremiah 29:11
  • 中文: 耶和华说：”我知道我向你们所怀的意念，是赐平安的意念，不是降灾祸的意念，要叫你们末后有指望。” - 耶利米书 29:11

第2天 / Day 2

  • English: “Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding.” - Proverbs 3:5
  • 中文: “你要专心仰赖耶和华，不可倚靠自己的聪明。” - 箴言 3:5

第3天 / Day 3

  • English: “I can do all this through him who gives me strength.” - Philippians 4:13
  • 中文: “我靠着那加给我力量的，凡事都能做。” - 腓立比书 4:13

第4天 / Day 4

  • English: “The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing.” - Psalm 23:1
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的牧者，我必不致缺乏。” - 诗篇 23:1

第5天 / Day 5

  • English: “Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go.” - Joshua 1:9
  • 中文: “你当刚强壮胆！不要惧怕，也不要惊惶，因为你无论往哪里去，耶和华你的神必与你同在。” - 约书亚记 1:9

第6天 / Day 6

  • English: “The Lord is my light and my salvation—whom shall I fear?” - Psalm 27:1
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的亮光，是我的拯救，我还怕谁呢？” - 诗篇 27:1

第7天 / Day 7

  • English: “But seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well.” - Matthew 6:33
  • 中文: “你们要先求他的国和他的义，这些东西都要加给你们了。” - 马太福音 6:33

第8天 / Day 8

  • English: “Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest.” - Matthew 11:28
  • 中文: “凡劳苦担重担的人，可以到我这里来，我就使你们得安息。” - 马太福音 11:28

第9天 / Day 9

  • English: “The name of the Lord is a strong tower; the righteous run to it and are safe.” - Proverbs 18:10
  • 中文: “耶和华的名是坚固台，义人奔入便得安稳。” - 箴言 18:10

第10天 / Day 10

  • English: “Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God.” - Philippians 4:6
  • 中文: “应当一无挂虑，只要凡事借着祷告、祈求和感谢，将你们所要的告诉神。” - 腓立比书 4:6

第11天 / Day 11

  • English: “And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose.” - Romans 8:28
  • 中文: “我们晓得万事都互相效力，叫爱神的人得益处，就是按他旨意被召的人。” - 罗马书 8:28

第12天 / Day 12

  • English: “Your word is a lamp for my feet, a light on my path.” - Psalm 119:105
  • 中文: “你的话是我脚前的灯，是我路上的光。” - 诗篇 119:105

第13天 / Day 13

  • English: “The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.” - Psalm 34:18
  • 中文: “耶和华靠近伤心的人，拯救灵性痛悔的人。” - 诗篇 34:18

第14天 / Day 14

  • English: “But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength. They will soar on wings like eagles; they will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint.” - Isaiah 40:31
  • 中文: “但那等候耶和华的，必从新得力。他们必如鹰展翅上腾，他们奔跑却不困倦，行走却不疲乏。” - 以赛亚书 40:31

第15天 / Day 15

  • English: “For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.” - John 3:16
  • 中文: “神爱世人，甚至将他的独生子赐给他们，叫一切信他的不致灭亡，反得永生。” - 约翰福音 3:16

第16天 / Day 16

  • English: “I have told you these things, so that in me you may have peace. In this world you will have trouble. But take heart! I have overcome the world.” - John 16:33
  • 中文: “我将这些事告诉你们，是要叫你们在我里面有平安。在世上你们有苦难，但你们可以放心，我已经胜了世界。” - 约翰福音 16:33

第17天 / Day 17

  • English: “Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!” - 2 Corinthians 5:17
  • 中文: “若有人在基督里，他就是新造的人，旧事已过，都变成新的了。” - 哥林多后书 5:17

第18天 / Day 18

  • English: “For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.” - Romans 6:23
  • 中文: “因为罪的工价乃是死；惟有神的恩赐，在我们的主基督耶稣里，乃是永生。” - 罗马书 6:23

第19天 / Day 19

  • English: “Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you.” - 1 Peter 5:7
  • 中文: “你们要将一切的忧虑卸给神，因为他顾念你们。” - 彼得前书 5:7

第20天 / Day 20

  • English: “The Lord is my strength and my shield; my heart trusts in him, and he helps me.” - Psalm 28:7
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的力量，是我的盾牌，我心里倚靠他，就得帮助。” - 诗篇 28:7

第21天 / Day 21

  • English: “Jesus looked at them and said, ‘With man this is impossible, but with God all things are possible.’” - Matthew 19:26
  • 中文: “耶稣看着他们说：’在人这是不能的，在神凡事都能。’” - 马太福音 19:26

第22天 / Day 22

  • English: “But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control.” - Galatians 5:22-23
  • 中文: “圣灵所结的果子就是仁爱、喜乐、和平、忍耐、恩慈、良善、信实、温柔、节制。” - 加拉太书 5:22-23

第23天 / Day 23

  • English: “The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still.” - Exodus 14:14
  • 中文: “耶和华必为你们争战，你们只管静默，不要作声。” - 出埃及记 14:14

第24天 / Day 24

  • English: “Do not let your hearts be troubled. You believe in God; believe also in me.” - John 14:1
  • 中文: “你们心里不要忧愁，你们信神，也当信我。” - 约翰福音 14:1

第25天 / Day 25

  • English: “He gives strength to the weary and increases the power of the weak.” - Isaiah 40:29
  • 中文: “疲乏的，他赐能力；软弱的，他加力量。” - 以赛亚书 40:29

第26天 / Day 26

  • English: “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom, and knowledge of the Holy One is understanding.” - Proverbs 9:10
  • 中文: “敬畏耶和华是智慧的开端，认识至圣者便是聪明。” - 箴言 9:10

第27天 / Day 27

  • English: “My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.” - 2 Corinthians 12:9
  • 中文: “我的恩典够你用的，因为我的能力是在人的软弱上显得完全。” - 哥林多后书 12:9

第28天 / Day 28

  • English: “Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.” - Galatians 6:9
  • 中文: “我们行善不可丧志，若不灰心，到了时候就要收成。” - 加拉太书 6:9

第29天 / Day 29

  • English: “The Lord is good, a refuge in times of trouble. He cares for those who trust in him.” - Nahum 1:7
  • 中文: “耶和华本为善，在患难的日子为人的保障，并且认得那些投靠他的人。” - 那鸿书 1:7

第30天 / Day 30

  • English: “But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God’s special possession, that you may declare the praises of him who called you out of darkness into his wonderful light.” - 1 Peter 2:9
  • 中文: “惟有你们是被拣选的族类，是有君尊的祭司，是圣洁的国度，是属神的子民，要叫你们宣扬那召你们出黑暗、入奇妙光明者的美德。” - 彼得前书 2:9

第31天 / Day 31

  • English: “So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand.” - Isaiah 41:10
  • 中文: “你不要害怕，因为我与你同在；不要惊惶，因为我是你的神。我必坚固你，我必帮助你，我必用我公义的右手扶持你。” - 以赛亚书 41:10

二月 / February

第32天 / Day 32

  • English: “Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud.” - 1 Corinthians 13:4
  • 中文: “爱是恒久忍耐，又有恩慈；爱是不嫉妒，爱是不自夸，不张狂。” - 哥林多前书 13:4

第33天 / Day 33

  • English: “Therefore, since we have been justified through faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ.” - Romans 5:1
  • 中文: “我们既因信称义，就借着我们的主耶稣基督得与神相和。” - 罗马书 5:1

第34天 / Day 34

  • English: “Your love, Lord, reaches to the heavens, your faithfulness to the skies.” - Psalm 36:5
  • 中文: “耶和华啊，你的慈爱上及诸天，你的信实达到穹苍。” - 诗篇 36:5

第35天 / Day 35

  • English: “The Lord is my rock, my fortress and my deliverer; my God is my rock, in whom I take refuge, my shield and the horn of my salvation, my stronghold.” - Psalm 18:2
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的岩石，我的山寨，我的救主，我的神，我的磐石，我所投靠的。他是我的盾牌，是拯救我的角，是我的高台。” - 诗篇 18:2

第36天 / Day 36

  • English: “But the Lord is faithful, and he will strengthen you and protect you from the evil one.” - 2 Thessalonians 3:3
  • 中文: “但主是信实的，要坚固你们，保护你们脱离那恶者。” - 帖撒罗尼迦后书 3:3

第37天 / Day 37

  • English: “For everyone who asks receives; the one who seeks finds; and to the one who knocks, the door will be opened.” - Matthew 7:8
  • 中文: “因为凡祈求的就得着，寻找的就寻见，叩门的就给他开门。” - 马太福音 7:8

第38天 / Day 38

  • English: “The Lord is gracious and righteous; our God is full of compassion.” - Psalm 116:5
  • 中文: “耶和华有恩惠，有公义，我们的神以怜悯为怀。” - 诗篇 116:5

第39天 / Day 39

  • English: “I have hidden your word in my heart that I might not sin against you.” - Psalm 119:11
  • 中文: “我将你的话藏在心里，免得我得罪你。” - 诗篇 119:11

第40天 / Day 40

  • English: “But you, Lord, are a compassionate and gracious God, slow to anger, abounding in love and faithfulness.” - Psalm 86:15
  • 中文: “主啊，你是有怜悯、有恩典的神，不轻易发怒，并有丰盛的慈爱和诚实。” - 诗篇 86:15

第41天 / Day 41

  • English: “The Lord is my strength and my song; he has become my salvation.” - Psalm 118:14
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的力量，是我的诗歌，他也成了我的拯救。” - 诗篇 118:14

第42天 / Day 42

  • English: “For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword, it penetrates even to dividing soul and spirit, joints and marrow; it judges the thoughts and attitudes of the heart.” - Hebrews 4:12
  • 中文: “神的道是活泼的，是有功效的，比一切两刃的剑更快，甚至魂与灵、骨节与骨髓，都能刺入、剖开，连心中的思念和主意都能辨明。” - 希伯来书 4:12

第43天 / Day 43

  • English: “But thanks be to God! He gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ.” - 1 Corinthians 15:57
  • 中文: “感谢神，使我们借着我们的主耶稣基督得胜。” - 哥林多前书 15:57

第44天 / Day 44

  • English: “The Lord is my light and my salvation—whom shall I fear? The Lord is the stronghold of my life—of whom shall I be afraid?” - Psalm 27:1
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的亮光，是我的拯救，我还怕谁呢？耶和华是我性命的保障，我还惧谁呢？” - 诗篇 27:1

第45天 / Day 45

  • English: “Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!” - 2 Corinthians 5:17
  • 中文: “若有人在基督里，他就是新造的人，旧事已过，都变成新的了。” - 哥林多后书 5:17

第46天 / Day 46

  • English: “And my God will meet all your needs according to the riches of his glory in Christ Jesus.” - Philippians 4:19
  • 中文: “我的神必照他荣耀的丰富，在基督耶稣里使你们一切所需用的都充足。” - 腓立比书 4:19

第47天 / Day 47

  • English: “The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing. He makes me lie down in green pastures, he leads me beside quiet waters.” - Psalm 23:1-2
  • 中文: “耶和华是我的牧者，我必不致缺乏。他使我躺卧在青草地上，领我在可安歇的水边。” - 诗篇 23:1-2

第48天 / Day 48

  • 中文: “耶和华靠近伤心的人，拯救灵性痛悔的人。” - 诗篇 34:18

  • 中文: “但你耶和华是我四围的盾牌，是我的荣耀，又是叫我抬起头来的。” - 诗篇 3:3

  • 中文: “耶和华是我的力量，是我的盾牌，我心里倚靠他，就得帮助。所以我心中欢乐，我必用诗歌颂赞他。” - 诗篇 28:7

  • 中文: “因为罪的工价乃是死；惟有神的恩赐，在我们的主基督耶稣里，乃是永生。” - 罗马书 6:23
